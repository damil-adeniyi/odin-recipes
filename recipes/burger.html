Burger

Description

A burger is a classic American dish consisting of a grilled or fried patty, typically made from ground beef, served in a sliced bun. It is often garnished with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and condiments like ketchup and mustard. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a favorite for many, perfect for barbecues, picnics, or casual dining.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 equal patties, making a slight indentation in the center of each.
  3. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the patties on the grill or skillet for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun and adding lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments as desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
